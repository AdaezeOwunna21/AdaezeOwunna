Creating a Sweet Online Presence: Building a Cake Website for a Small Business

Evelyn Cakes and Bakes is a small upcoming bakery business.
The owner specializes in making cakes, designing events, and more
for the city of Houston. She wanted to expand her presence by building
and online website.She already had an Instagram, but she also wanted to
be more known in the digital marketplace. The owner created a website but
but it wasn't gaining any traction. By redesigning her webite, more customers
flocked to the business and wanted to buy cakes and more.

The Website Lacked Personality and Branding

User feedback indicated that the website needed its own personal branding that
shows what type of business it represents. One user complained about seeing the
wood,“I wouldn’t even know that they sell cake because the first picture I see
is a piece of wood”. The website needed to feel like a cake website and show
more personality.

Design

Whisking Up a Delightful Cake Website

The owner didn’t have a lot of content to share, so the best idea was to create
a single one-page website, that had all the user needed to know about this upcoming
cake business. The website also lacked personality, so adding pictures of cakes,
drawings, and a more playful type of font added to the user's understanding of what
the business was all about. Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript the website was created.

Conclusion

Putting the Cherry on Top of the Finishing Touches

The users loved the new designs of the website and the layout, “I love how at home,
I feel when I come across this website like my mom is baking a cake for me”. Creating
a website that makes people feel connected to the brand is most important. With this
new interface, users understood more about the business and were more inclined to want
to work with the client.
